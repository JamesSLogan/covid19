Restrictions that are changing

As of June 15, 2021, the Governor is terminating the executive orders that put into place the Stay Home Order and the Blueprint for a Safer Economy. He is also phasing out the vast majority of executive actions put in place since March 2020 as part of the pandemic response, leaving in place a handful of provisions that facilitate the ongoing recovery.

The public health officer has issued a new public health order effective June 15, superseding all prior health orders. The new order has limited restrictions, only related to masking and mega-events, as well as settings serving children and youth pending an expected update to the K-12 schools guidance by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.

The public health officer has also issued new guidance on masks that is effective on June 15, and he will end the Travel Advisory.
