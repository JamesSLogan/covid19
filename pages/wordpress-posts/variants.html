Variants in California

California genetically sequences a sample of positive COVID-19 tests to determine their variant. Knowing what variants are circulating in California informs public health and clinical action.

This chart shows the sample of sequenced positive tests where the variant was identified. Sequencing can only be done at specialized labs. It generally occurs several days or more after a test is identified as are positive for COVID-19. Not all positive COVID-19 tests are genetically sequenced.
