Million-Dollar Shot – California’s Vaccine Incentive Program

Get vaccinated, get rewarded

Not yet vaccinated against COVID-19? Listen up! Get your shots and you will receive a $50 incentive card as a “thank you” for helping us beat the pandemic in California.

Already vaccinated or about to be? Great! You’ll have a shot at winning $50,000 or a grand prize of $1.5 million!

$15 million in prizes

$50,000 Fridays

$50 cards

How you can win

$15 million in prizes

Our biggest “thank you” yet to Californians who have received their COVID-19 vaccine. Ten winners will be selected on June 15 to receive $1.5 million each — prizes totaling $15 million! All Californians who have had at least one COVID-19 shot will automatically be entered.

You’re eligible to win $1.5 million if you:

  • Live in California,
  • Are aged 12 and older,
  • And have received at least a first shot of your COVID-19 vaccine.

$50,000 Fridays

If you’ve already been vaccinated, we want to thank you. Everyone with at least one COVID-19 shot in the state of California will be automatically entered to win big! Thirty winners will take home $50,000. Fifteen people will be selected on Friday, June 4 and Friday, June 11.

You’re eligible to win on $50,000 Fridays if you:

  • Live in California,
  • Are aged 12 and older,
  • And have received at least a first shot of your COVID-19 vaccine.

$50 incentive cards

Not vaccinated? Now is your time to call the shot. Starting on May 26, the first two million Californians to complete their COVID-19 vaccination will receive:

  • A $50 virtual incentive card (which can be spent online, in-store where major debit cards are accepted, or added to a mobile wallet to be used to shop in-stores that accept mobile wallets),
  • Or a $50 grocery gift card from Kroger (which includes Ralphs, Food 4 Less and Foods Co.),
  • Or a $50 grocery gift card from Albertsons (which includes Safeway, Albertsons, Vons, Pavilions and Andronico’s Community Markets).

Available while supplies last.

You’re eligible to receive a $50 incentive card if you:

  • Live in California,
  • Are aged 12 and older,
  • And get vaccinated starting on or after May 26.

You will also be entered to win in the cash drawings!

Get vaccinated

Ready to take your shot? Go to myturn.ca.gov or call the CA COVID-19 Hotline at 1-833-422-4255 to find a vaccination site near you. Many allow you to walk in without an appointment.

You do not have to book through My Turn to qualify for this incentive. You are eligible if you get vaccinated at any site in California that reports to the statewide vaccination registry (CAIR), including in-home vaccination services.

Get vaccinated at myturn

Get the code

If you get vaccinated on or after May 26, you’ll get a redemption code by text or email within 7-10 days after your last vaccination. For those receiving a two-dose series, your incentive card will be set aside until your completed second shot.

  • If you do not have a mobile phone or email address, you can receive a physical card by calling 1-833-993-3873.
  • Those without a permanent address can also call 1-833-993-3873 to coordinate delivery of their incentive card.

Pick your reward

Choose your card! We’ll send you instructions.

It takes up to five days (including weekends) to receive the incentive card of choice, once the code is redeemed.

If you are younger than 18, you will need to have a parent or guardian complete the incentive card selection process for you, unless you are an emancipated minor.

Take your shot

Questions and answers

$50 incentive cards

How long will the incentive cards last? When will the program run out?

The incentive card program is only eligible for the first two million individuals to begin and end their vaccination process after May 26.

How is this being paid for?

This program is being paid for by the State of California.

If someone got their first shot before the incentive card program started, and then get their second shot after it started, are they still eligible?

No, they are not eligible to receive an incentive card.

Are Californians who already completed their full vaccination prior to the program eligible for the incentive card?

No. The incentive card program is intended to motivate the remaining Californians to get vaccinated before the state fully reopens on June 15.

How long after redeeming the incentive card does it take to receive it?

It takes up to five business days to receive the incentive card. If you have not received your card after five business days, call 1-833-993-3873.

How will people prove they’ve had both shots? What if someone tries to cheat with a fake vaccine card?

COVID-19 vaccines are tracked in the California Immunization Registry (CAIR) and incentive cards will be awarded based on those records.

How do I book a COVID-19 vaccination appointment?

You can schedule appointments or view walk-in clinics at myturn.ca.gov or via other resources provided by the CDC or local health departments. Appointments can also be made by calling the California COVID-19 Hotline at 1-833-422-4255.

What if I got the shots, but did not receive my code?

If you do not receive a code after 14 days, call 833-993-3873.

What if I get my shots outside of California’s My Turn system?

This program applies to all vaccination sites in California that use their electronic health records or any site using My Turn, including vaccinations administered through at-home services. All providers administering COVID-19 vaccines are required to report administered doses to the state. The only clinics not eligible are federal Veterans Affairs clinics.

What if I opted to not share my data with Information Immunization Systems (IIS)?

The data is still reported to the state, so you would still be eligible for the incentive.

Is there a choice of incentive cards?

Yes, there are three choices:

  1. Virtual Mastercard®: Not an ATM card so it cannot be cashed out. It can be redeemed online where Debit Mastercard® is accepted. This is a virtual incentive card and can be added to a phone mobile wallet.
  2. Kroger (which includes Ralphs, Food 4 Less and Foods Co.): Printing the incentive card is recommended as not all grocery stores can scan the incentive card.
  3. Albertsons (which includes Safeway, Albertsons, Vons and Andronico’s Community Markets): Requires recipient to print barcode to redeem for in-store purchases.

Available while supplies last.

Does my redemption code ever expire?

Yes. Redemption codes expire 90 days after you  receive it. The incentive cards themselves have different expiration dates. The Virtual Mastercard® expires after 12 months of receipt. The Kroger or Albertsons card never expires.
