How it works

Now’s the time to call the shot. Starting on May XX, the first two million Californians to complete their COVID-19 vaccination will get a $50 incentive for Kroger, (which includes Ralphs, Food 4 Less and Foods Co.), Albertsons (which includes Safeway, Albertsons, Vons and Andronico’s Community Markets) or Virtual Mastercard®.

Who’s eligible

  • People who live in California
  • People aged 12 and older
  • People who got their vaccine starting on or after May XX (must complete vaccination)

If you are younger than 18, you will need to have a parent or guardian complete the incentive card selection process for you, unless you are an emancipated minor.

Get vaccinated

Go to myturn.ca.gov or call the CA COVID-19 Hotline at 1-833-422-4255 to find a vaccination site near you. Many allow you to walk in without an appointment.

You do not have to book through My Turn to qualify. You can get your shot at any vaccination site in California that uses electronic health records, including in-home vaccination services.

Get vaccinated at myturn

Get the code

  • 7-10 days after your last vaccination, you’ll get a redemption code by text or email.
    • If you do not have a mobile phone or email address, you can receive a physical card by calling 1-833-993-3873.
    • Those without a permanent address can also call 1-833-993-3873 to coordinate delivery of their incentive card.
  • It takes up to five days (including weekends) to receive the incentive card once the code is redeemed. 
    • If you still haven’t received your prepaid card after five days, please call 1-833-993-3873.

Questions and answers

How long will the You Call the Shot California program last? When will the program run out?

The program is only eligible for the first two million individuals to begin and end their vaccination process after May XX.

How is this being paid for?

This program is being paid for by the State of California.

If someone got their first shot before the incentive program started, and then get their second shot after it started, are they still eligible?

No. The You Call the Shot California program is only open to Californians who start their vaccination process after May XX.

Are Californians who already completed their full vaccination prior to the program eligible for the incentive card?

No. This program is intended to motivate the remaining Californians to get vaccinated before the state fully reopens on June 15.

How long after redeeming the incentive card does it take to receive it?

It takes up to 5 business days to receive the prepaid card. If you have not received your card after 5 business days call 1-833-993-3873.

How will people prove they’ve had both shots? What if someone tries to cheat with a fake vaccine card?

COVID-19 vaccines are tracked in the California Immunization Registry (CAIR) and cards will be awarded based on those records.

How do I book a COVID-19 vaccination appointment?

You can schedule appointments or view walk-in clinics at myturn.ca.gov or via other resources provided by the CDC or local health departments. Appointments can also be made by calling the California COVID-19 Hotline at 1-833-422-4255.

What if I got the shots, but did not receive my code?

If you do not receive a code after 14 days, call 833-993-3873.

What if I get my shots outside of California’s My Turn system?

This program applies to all vaccination sites in California that use their electronic health records or any site using My Turn, including vaccinations through at-home services. All providers administering COVID-19 vaccine are required to report administered doses to the state. The only clinics not eligible are federal Veterans Affairs clinics.

What if I opted to not share my data with Information Immunization Systems (IIS)?

The data is still reported to the state, so you would still be eligible for the incentive.

Is there a choice of incentive cards?

Yes, there are three choices:

  1. Virtual Mastercard®: Not an ATM card so it cannot be cashed out. It can be redeemed online where Debit Mastercard® is accepted. This is a virtual prepaid card and can be added to a phone mobile  wallet.
  2. Kroger (which includes Ralphs, Food 4 Less and Foods Co.): Printing the gift card is recommended as not all grocery stores can scan the gift card.
  3. Albertsons (which includes Safeway, Albertsons, Vons and Andronico’s Community Markets): Requires recipient to print barcode to redeem for in-store purchases.

Does my redemption code ever expire?

Yes. Redemption codes expire 90 days after you  receive it. The incentive cards themselves have different expiration dates. The Virtual Mastercard® expires after 12 months of receipt. The Kroger or Albertsons card never expires.
