You Call the Shot CA

Get vaccinated, get rewarded

Californians who still arenot vaccinated against COVID-19, listen up. Get your shot and you can get a $50 gift card as a “thank you” from the state! Help us beat the pandemic, and get vaccinated.

How it works

Now’s the time to call your shot. The first two million Californians to get a COVID-19 vaccination shot on May 24 or after will get a $50 Vons, Safeway, Albertsons, or Mastercard prepaid card.

Who’s eligible

  • People who live in California
  • 12 and older
  • Vaccinated on or after May 24

If you’re 12 to 17 years old, a parent or guardian will need to complete your claim.
