​CDPH Rolls Out Statewide COVID-19 Vaccine Outreach for Black and African American Audiences

Ahead of expanded eligibility for the COVID-19 vaccine on April 15 for all Californians aged 16+, the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) is rolling out tailored outreach for Black and African American communities across the state.
